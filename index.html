Features

Fifteen tools. One app. All yours.

A complete budgeting system designed for everyday life.

Photo Diary

Remember the moments behind every expense. Snap receipts, capture meals, document the "why" behind every peso spent. Build a visual timeline of your financial life.

Wallet Tracking

Separate your BDO savings, GCash, and daily cash into individual wallets. Always know exactly which pocket has what — without opening four apps.

Bills Manager

Meralco. PLDT. Rent. Globe postpaid. Log them once, mark them paid when done. No reminders from your landlord required.

Savings Vaults

Create dedicated funds for future goals. Emergency fund, travel, gadgets. Track progress visually and stay motivated with every peso you save.

Transaction Timeline

Scroll back to any day and see exactly what you spent, where, and from which wallet. Filter by Food, Transport, Bills, or any custom category.

Offline First

Logging your jeepney fare in a dead zone? Your groceries at the wet market? No signal needed. Every feature works, always — because nothing relies on a server.

Utang Tracker

Track money you owe and money owed to you. Record payments, see repayment progress, and never forget an informal debt again. Built for Filipino utang culture.

Quick Entry Templates

One-tap shortcuts for everyday expenses. Jeepney, sari-sari store, Grab — log your most common spends in seconds. Fully customizable in Settings.

Cashflow Forecast

See your projected balance for the next 30 days based on your spending patterns and upcoming bills. Avoid surprises before they happen.

Pera Report

Your monthly money story. See your spending personality, top categories, biggest expense, and savings rate — all beautifully presented.

Wallet Transfer

Move money between Cash, GCash, Maya, and bank accounts with optional fees. Edit transfers, filter by type, and track every movement in the transaction log.

Global Search

Find anything across expenses, income, bills, transfers, and vaults with Ctrl+K. Grouped results, instant filtering, and click-to-navigate — works offline.

CSV Export

Download your complete transaction history as a spreadsheet. Excel-compatible with UTF-8 encoding. Your data, your format — free for everyone.

Budget vs. Actual

See how your spending compares to budget limits at a glance. Color-coded progress bars on the dashboard show which categories are on track and which are over.

Smart Notifications

Get alerted when a wallet runs low, a bill is due tomorrow, or a budget category nears its limit. All local, all private, no push server needed.

Fast and Reliable

Instant access to your finances. No loading, no lag.

"What if I clear my browser cache?"
Your data is stored in your browser's localStorage and IndexedDB — not the cache. It persists unless you manually clear site data. We recommend exporting a backup regularly.
"Can I use this on two devices?"
SINKPESO is single-device by design. Your data never leaves the device you're on — that's the point. Export your data as JSON to migrate manually.
"Where do my photos go?"
Photos attached to expenses are stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing leaves your device.

The Story

This Started With a Spreadsheet.

I wasn’t a programmer.

I was just someone trying to make sense of where my money was going.

Like many people, I bounced between notes, spreadsheets, screenshots, and budgeting apps that never quite felt right.

Some were too complicated.

Some wanted accounts and cloud syncing.

Others didn’t match how I actually managed pesos, cash, e-wallets, bills, and savings.

So I started building my own.

One feature became two.

Two became ten.

Eventually, that project became SINKPESO.

An offline-first budgeting app built around a simple idea:

you should be in control of your money — and your data.
