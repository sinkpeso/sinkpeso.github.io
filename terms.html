Description of Service

SINKPESO is a free, offline-first personal finance tracker. It lets you log expenses, manage wallets, create savings goals, attach photos to transactions, and track bills. The app runs entirely in your browser and stores all data locally on your device.

A premium license is available as an optional upgrade. It unlocks unlimited wallets, unlimited savings vaults, encrypted backups, recurring expenses, PDF reports, and multi-currency support. The free version is fully functional for everyday use.
